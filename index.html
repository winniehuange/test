[Project Brief]

I want to create a platform which can let people who like reading and enjoy sharing share their bookshelf at home, and through platform to open bookstore so that people can rent books to someone who want to read books.
Because of using mutually beneficial way to share books, one get money, another get books content, comment and some personal view, I prefer to rent book model.
Therefore, I begain to design this product, and name it「ShaRead」.
